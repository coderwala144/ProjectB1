Northern India (Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh)

  • Wheat:Yellow rust, karnal bunt, loose smut
  • Rice:Bacterial leaf blight, blast, sheath blight
  • Cotton:: Cotton leaf curl virus, bacterial blight
  • Sugarcane:Red rot, smut, wilt

    • Eastern India (West Bengal, Bihar, Odisha)

  • Rice:Yellow rust, karnal bunt, loose smut
  • Jute:: Stem rot, anthracnose, root rot
  • Vegetables:: Late blight in potatoes, downy mildew in cucurbits
  • Tea:Blister blight, grey blight, root rot

    • Western India (Gujarat, Maharashtra, Rajasthan)

  • Cotton: Alternaria leaf spot, grey mildew
  • Groundnut: Collar rot, leaf spots, rust
  • Mango: Powdery mildew, anthracnose
  • Bajra/Pearl millet: Downy mildew, ergot, smut

    • Southern India (Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh)

  • Rice:Blast, sheath rot, bacterial leaf streak
  • Coconut: Bud rot, stem bleeding, leaf rot
  • Banana: Sigatoka leaf spot, Panama wilt, bunchy top
  • Spices:Black pepper quick wilt, cardamom mosaic virus

    • Central India (Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh)

  • Soybean:Rust, yellow mosaic virus, anthracnose
  • Pulses: Fusarium wilt in chickpea, sterility mosaic in pigeonpea
  • Wheat:Loose smut, powdery mildew
  • Rice: Brown spot, blast

    • Northeastern India (Assam, Arunachal Pradesh, Manipur)

  • Rice: Blast, brown spot, sheath blight
  • Tea: Blister blight, red rust, black rot
  • Citrus:Citrus greening (Huanglongbing), canker
  • Ginger:Bacterial wilt, soft rot, leaf spot
